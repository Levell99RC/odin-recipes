Fried Rice

Description

An easy and delicious dish of fried rice with whatever you have available!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of day-old rice. (You can spread freshly cooked rice on a sheet tray and dry out in the oven on low temp.
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 medium-sized yellow onion
  • 2 medium carrots
  • Half cup of frozen peas
  • About 2 tbsp of soy sauce
  • 3 stalks of green onions/scallions
  • Feel free to add any other vegetables or protein. Just dice them finely!

Steps

  1. Peel and finely dice all vegetables (and proteins).
  2. Thinly slice the green onions.
  3. Scramble eggs first and pan and break it up into little bits. Remove from pan and reserve for later.
  4. Add oil and saute vegetables. Add proteins here as well if you are using them.
  5. Once fully cooked (about 5-8 minutes), add in rice and begin tossing around.
  6. Add back in the eggs and mix it around.
  7. Add in the soy sauce and toss to combine.
  8. After everything is combined, remove and serve. Garnish with the scallions.
