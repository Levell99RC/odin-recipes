Cheeseburger

Description

Learn how to make an all-American classic: the cheeseburger. There's never a wrong time for one!

Ingredients

  • Potato buns
  • Ground beef. Make sure it has at least 20% fat content.
  • Butterleaf lettuce
  • A large, beefsteak tomato
  • A red onion
  • A slice of your favorite cheese (I will be using cheddar)
  • Pickle slices
  • Condiments of your choosing (I'll be using ketchup)

Steps

  1. Remove 2 leaves of butterleaf lettuce and clean.
  2. Cut a thick slice of tomato and lightly salt it.
  3. Thinly slices rings or strips of red onion.
  4. Take ground beef and shape into patty. I recommend making it larger than the bun and about 1/2 in thick.
  5. Set pan on medium and toast the buns.
  6. Remove the buns once done and raise the heat to high. Add in some oil.
  7. While the pan gets hot, add sauce to both buns. Stack lettuce, onion, and tomato on bottom bun (in that order).
  8. Once the pan is hot, lay the patty in. Season with salt and pepper on the exposed side.
  9. After roughly 3 minutes, flip and season the other side. Top with cheese slice to melt.
  10. After another 3 minutes, remove and either rest to lose some excess juice or place on sandwich.
  11. Top the patty with the pickles and enjoy!
